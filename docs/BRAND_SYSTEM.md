# Overlord Brand System

_Source: Artist-tower brand consult + existing-DNA audit (2026-07-10). Grounded in the Workshop Preview.png, not invented._

## Essence
Overlord is an old-world overseer's command console for RimWorld — near-black ledger ground, one solitary gold, square RimWorld edges, a star-in-circle seal of authority, and a targeting reticle that marks every colonist under viewer command. Voice is terse, dry-wry, and RimWorld-native: the overseer register, never cheerful, drama carried by the reticle and gold command-lines, not by copy.

## Logo
- **Concept:** The STAR-IN-CIRCLE seal from the Preview (a 5-point solid star inscribed in a gold ring) is the logo seed — the ownable silhouette, not the letter O. Reconstruct it on a real construction grid (Artist logo doc 2): one primary circle = the ring; a 5-point star on a concentric circle with points pushed ~2% past their circle so they optically touch the ring. Because it lives gold-on-near-black (#090b0a), treat gold-on-dark AS the knockout/reversed case and thicken the ring stroke ~10% so it doesn't thin out on the dark tab. Deliver as SVG source, not an upscaled PNG. This REPLACES the placeholder gold O-ring (Textures/UI/Overlord.png) — a bare ring is indistinct in the Twitch/RimWorld-mod icon grid; the star geometry carries the ID and survives the solid-black squint test.
- **Icon brief (32px):** 32px in-game main-tab button and Workshop icon: a filled near-black #090b0a rounded-2px square (matches the --radius:0px/tight brand and RimWorld's chunky tab buttons) with the gold star-in-circle occupying ~80% of the canvas — NOT a small star floating on transparency (fill the ground, app-icon rule). Ring stroke thickened for the dark ground; solid gold #d2a95d star, bright-gold #f0cf82 optional 1px inner highlight only if it survives. DROP the line-diamond-line deco rules and the reticle corner-brackets entirely at this size (they alias away). 16px favicon: solid gold star on a near-black disc, ring only if it still reads. Author in a size ladder 128->32->16, every edge on a pixel boundary, 2 colors max.
- **Wordmark:** OVERLORD set in a HEAVY CONDENSED SLAB-SERIF, tight tracking (~ -0.01 to -0.02em), all-caps, in brand gold #d2a95d — matching the Preview exactly (the Preview wordmark is a Clarendon/Egyptian slab with bold bracketed terminals, NOT a contrast serif). Named direction: Clarendon / Sentinel / Roboto Slab-Heavy family — specifically a condensed Egyptian slab. Do NOT use Fraunces (it is a high-contrast display serif and will not match the even-weight slab terminals seen in the Preview). The wordmark is the primary type moment; the UI body face is separate (see typography).
- **Lockups:** Four committed SVG variants: (1) FULL BADGE — star-in-circle emblem + line-diamond-line deco rules + OVERLORD wordmark + tagline; Workshop hero and GitHub README ONLY. (2) WORDMARK-ONLY — condensed slab OVERLORD; wide headers and the viewer web-UI top bar. (3) ICON-ONLY — star-in-circle; the 32px in-game tab button, favicon, avatars. (4) MONO-WHITE knockout for over-screenshot/video, MONO-BLACK for engrave/fax. Clear-space = 1x the ring diameter. Minimum icon size 32px; below that only the simplified favicon variant. NEVER ship the full badge (deco rules + wordmark + tagline) at <=32px — it muds out; the deco rules and reticle brackets belong ONLY to large marketing lockups.

## Palette
- **Primary:** Near-black ground #090b0a (with surface steps #0f1210 / #141816 / #1a201d) + ONE solitary gold accent #d2a95d, bright-gold #f0cf82 for emphasis/highlights. Wood #3e2918/#8a5e30 and parchment #e8d4a8 (text #2a1d10) are the single texture/material family, not a second accent color.
- **Secondary:** Semantic-only, never chrome: green #82c58c (good/gains/food), red #d47768 (bad/downed/loss), yellow #e3bf68 (warning/joy). These carry meaning exclusively — never used decoratively. NO purple, NO purple-to-blue gradient, NO second decorative accent (dodges the #1 2026 slop tell).
- **Distribution:** 70% near-black ground / 25% gold (wordmark, emblem, borders, reticle, command-lines) / 5% semantic red-green-yellow. Gold is the only decorative accent; keep it solitary against the dark field. Lock --radius:0px as a brand constant with a 'DO NOT CHANGE — RimWorld square edges' comment.

## Typography
- **Display:** Clarendon / Sentinel / condensed Egyptian heavy slab-serif (e.g. Clarendon Bold-Condensed, Sentinel Black, or Roboto Slab at max weight condensed) — matches the Preview's bracketed even-weight slab terminals. Set --font-display and --font-name to THIS, all-caps, tight tracking. Explicitly NOT Fraunces (contrast serif, wrong terminals) and NOT IBM Plex Sans (the current slop tell).
- **Body:** IBM Plex Mono for UI/data/viewer-names/command-labels — the console/ledger register reinforces the 'command console' read and is already loaded via --font-mono, so the change is additive. Source Sans 3 as the readable-prose fallback for any longer UI text only.
- **Mono:** IBM Plex Mono (primary UI/data face) with JetBrains Mono retained for code/monospaced tabular data.
- **Rationale:** The #1 brand gap is that --font-ui, --font-display, AND --font-name are all IBM Plex Sans — a dead-giveaway AI-slop face, and a generic sans cosplaying the Preview's distinctive slab. Fix: split the three vars. Display/name = a condensed heavy SLAB matching the Preview wordmark (drop Plex Sans entirely). Body/UI = IBM Plex MONO for the ledger/console voice. This gives the extreme weight/contrast jump the de-slop guide demands (slab 800+ vs mono 400) and keeps ONE existing Plex var (Mono), so the change is additive not a rewrite.

## Voice
Overlord speaks as the overseer's ledger/console: TERSE (verb-noun labels, no filler), OVERSEER (dry command-register), RIMWORLD-NATIVE (reuse the game's own words — Prioritize, Draft, Haul, Tend), and DRY-WRY (humor lives in the colony's brutality and the overseer conceit, NEVER punching at the viewer). Energy is matter-of-fact — the gold reticle and command-lines carry the drama, so copy stays understated with ZERO exclamation points anywhere. Tone flexes by context but voice is fixed: command accepted = flat ack, colonist dies = dry not celebratory, connection lost = calm and blameless. Rewrites: 'Your command was successfully sent to the colonist!' -> 'Order received.'  |  'Oopsie, your pawn died!' -> 'Colonist downed. That was your call.'  |  'Loading...' -> 'Reading the ledger...' (specific: syncing colonist list). Glossary is locked: colonist (never pawn/unit in viewer copy), order (never action/task), session (never game/stream/match), overseer (the streamer), viewers (the audience), the colony (collective).

## Do NOT do
- Do NOT set the display/wordmark in Fraunces, a contrast serif, or any generic sans (Inter/Roboto/Plex Sans/Space Grotesk) — the Preview wordmark is a heavy CONDENSED EGYPTIAN SLAB and the type must match it. Plex Sans across all three font vars is the current #1 slop tell being fixed.
- Do NOT keep the placeholder gold O-ring as the icon — a bare ring is indistinct in the mod-icon grid. The star-in-circle geometry is the ownable mark.
- Do NOT ship the full badge (deco rules + wordmark + tagline) at <=32px, and do NOT put the deco rules or reticle corner-brackets inside dense product panels — those are large-lockup / marketing devices only.
- Do NOT introduce a second decorative accent color, purple, purple-to-blue gradients, gradient-fill text, glassmorphism, or hover/glow shadows. Gold stays solitary; red/green/yellow are semantic-only and never used for chrome.
- Do NOT inflate border-radius past 2px — --radius:0px is a load-bearing RimWorld-console brand constant; mark it DO NOT CHANGE.
- Do NOT stack uppercase + letter-spacing across multiple hierarchy tiers — reserve tracked-caps for the ONE Preview-quoting tagline; everything else sentence case.
- Do NOT add marketing subtitles, exclamation points, cheerful acks, giant empty-state icons above text, or per-item colored icons. Empty/error states = one factual line in the overseer register; the reticle is the brand mark, not empty-state decoration.
- Do NOT over-card the in-game RimWorld tab (RimWorld lists are already dense) — brand it via the header emblem, gold accent, and reticle motif, not by wrapping every row in a bordered card.
- Do NOT let the viewer-facing copy drift the glossary — colonist not pawn/unit, order not action/task, session not game/stream, overseer not host/admin. Mixing registers makes the product read schizophrenic.
- Do NOT add a second texture/pattern family — wood + parchment is the single material language; don't layer a geometric pattern on top.
